## üìÑ Project Overview
The research focuses on the "Corporate Turnaround" and "Swadeshi" market strategy of Patanjali Foods. It evaluates how the company transitioned from insolvency to a leading FMCG powerhouse by 2026.

### Key Analysis Sections:
1.  **Company Overview:** Integration of Ayurvedic principles with industrial scale.
2.  **Brief History:** From Ruchi Soya (1986) to the Patanjali acquisition (2019).
3.  **SWOT Analysis:** Identification of competitive advantages and promoter-linked risks.
4.  **Strategic Framework:** Analysis of Cost Leadership and Asset-Light distribution.
5.  **Market Landscape:** Competitive positioning against HUL, Adani Wilmar, and ITC.
6.  **Growth Trajectory:** Future outlook on the Nutraceuticals and Palm Oil segments.
